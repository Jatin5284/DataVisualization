India, located in South Asia, is the world's second-most populous 
country, known for its rich cultural diversity and history. It is a 
federal parliamentary democratic republic, consisting of 28 states and 
8 Union territories, with New Delhi as its capital. India boasts a 
rapidly growing economy, being one of the largest globally, driven by 
sectors like technology, manufacturing, and services. It is home to 
several world-renowned landmarks such as the Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World 
Heritage site, and the spiritual city of Varanasi. India's vibrant 
traditions, festivals, and cuisines reflect its vast and varied 
cultural heritage.
